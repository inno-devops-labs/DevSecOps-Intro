=== Comprehensive Tool Comparison ===

Terraform Scanning Results:
  - tfsec: 53 findings
  - Checkov: 78 findings
  - Terrascan: 22 findings

Pulumi Scanning Results (KICS): 6 findings
Ansible Scanning Results (KICS): 10 findings

High-level conclusions:
- Checkov had the widest Terraform policy coverage.
- tfsec gave the cleanest fast-feedback Terraform report.
- Terrascan produced fewer but still useful compliance-oriented Terraform findings.
- KICS worked for Pulumi YAML and Ansible secrets, but coverage was incomplete compared with manual review of the source.

Recommended CI/CD strategy:
- Pull request fast gate: tfsec on Terraform diffs
- Merge gate: Checkov across Terraform and other supported IaC
- Scheduled compliance scan: Terrascan
- YAML/IaC consistency layer: KICS for Pulumi YAML and Ansible
- Manual review / supplemental tooling: Pulumi Python and risky Ansible automation patterns
