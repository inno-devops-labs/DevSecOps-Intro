### When would you pick each?
2-3 sentences each:
- When does Syft+Grype's **decoupled** model win? 
 I would choose Syft + Grype when I need a reusable SBOM that can be scanned multiple times over its lifecycle. The CycloneDX SBOM generated by Syft becomes an artifact that can later be attached to the image as an attestation and signed with Cosign. This approach is especially valuable for incident response because newly disclosed CVEs can be checked against an existing SBOM without rebuilding or re-pulling the image.
- When does Trivy's **all-in-one** win?
 I would choose Trivy when I want a simple CI/CD workflow with minimal configuration. A single tool can scan container images, detect vulnerabilities, inspect IaC configurations, find exposed secrets, and identify security misconfigurations. This makes it a good choice for fast feedback in developer pipelines and smaller teams that prefer an integrated security scanner.
